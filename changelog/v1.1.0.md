# Echocraft 1.1.0 – The Resonance Update
The echoes grow louder. This update refines the core of EchoCraft, improving performance and current features, polishing old textures, fixing bugs, and introducing a brand-new weapon born from the depths of the Deep Dark.

## Technical Changes
- Rework JavaScript code to align with the latest API version.
- General performance and stability improvements across all systems.

## Gameplay Changes
### Warden Heart
- Now deals true damage that ignores armour entirely to match the behaviour of the vanilla sonic boom.
- Players wearing the Warden Heart are now hidden from the locator bar.

### Echo Sword
- Added a dedicated button for MCPE players to strike lightning more easily.
- Added a visual cooldown to help players know when they can strike again.

### Echo Pickaxe
- Increased the Night Vision ability duration from 15 seconds to 30 seconds.
- Added lit redstone ore, lit deepslate redstone ore, warped nylium, crimson nylium, and all the new copper blocks from the Copper Age Drop to the list of minable blocks.

### Echo Axe
- Added all of the shelves to the list of Minable blocks.

### All Echo Tools
- The Echo Pickaxe, Sword, Shovel, Axe and Hoe are now fire-resistant.
  - They will no longer burn or be destroyed by fire or lava, much like Netherite.
  - ⚠️*Known Issue*: Due to a Minecraft bug, fire-resistant items appear invisible when thrown in fire or lava. Don't panic, your items are still there and can be picked up.
- Changed the rarity of The Echo Pickaxe, Sword, Shovel, Axe and Hoe from common to rare
- The Echo Pickaxe, Sword, Shovel, Axe and Hoe won't lose durability anymore when mining. But they will still lose durability when hitting an entity.
  - ⚠️Once Mojang fixes the unbreaking enchantment on custom tools, I will be reversing this. This is a temporary solution.

## Textures
- Updated the Ghost Step Potion texture.
- Updated the Warden Heart texture.
- Removed an extra pixel from a few textures.

## New Item
### Chakrams
A weapon that cuts deep through the dark and through your enemies.
- Using 4 Iron Ingots and a Broken Soul in a crafting table gives 4 Chakrams.
- Functions as a throwable projectile, similar to a snowball.
- Has a one-second cooldown
- On impact:
  - Spawns blue fire particles around the hit entity.
  - Deals 5 attack damage (2 ½ hearts).

## Bug fixes
- Fixed an issue where Broken Souls could randomly drop (Hopefully it's fixed)
- Fixed Soul Dust not dropping correctly.
- Echo Tools can now be repaired with Broken Souls in an anvil as intended.

## Notes

More features will be added in the next update regarding the Chakrams.
